Can I hire permanently?

The purpose of TEZISH is to connect businesses that need short-term workers with workers who want to work on short-term jobs.

Furthermore, the vast majority of TEZISH employees are not looking for a permanent job, according to what they told us in interviews, as many are either students or work as a second job on the platform.

However, you are completely free to offer permanent jobs to short-term TEZISH employees in your company. We even recommend that you mention that you can offer a permanent job when posting a temporary job.

How do I know that the incoming employee is good?

You can view the rating and administrator comments of the employee during his tenure at TEZISH. In addition, TEZISH employees allow an employee who wants to work at TEZISH to the platform only if he passes each of the following 3 stages:
1. Face-to-face interview with the employee
2. Data shared by the employee: Profile picture, work experience, address, foreign language, education, reference number, ID card information.
3. TEZISH employees decide whether or not to allow the employee to the platform based on the interview and shared information.

How is the payment calculated?

Based on our experience in this field, we recommend that the hourly payment be at least 1.5 AZN. However, if you need a worker urgently (less than 6 hours), then we recommend that you offer 10-20-30% extra depending on how urgent it is. However, TEZISH does not require any payment from you. You are completely free to offer whatever payment you want for whatever term you want.

What should I do if the employee does not come?

In our experience, employees who have been hired but who refuse to work for personal reasons must notify us 12 hours before starting work. Therefore, in many cases we will already be aware and the 2nd most suitable employee of your choice has been notified, who will start immediately.
If the employee does not come to work without informing us and you, please notify us immediately so that we can eliminate this problem as soon as possible.

How do I opt out of an ad or an employee?

You are completely free to refuse an advertisement or an employee working in your company for a certain period of time at any time.

1. Rejecting the advertisement: If you want to refuse the recruitment advertisement, please inform us as soon as possible so that the advertisement is removed from the platform and the applying employees are informed.

2. Rejecting an employee: If you have selected an employee to start or if the employee has worked for a certain period of time and you want to opt out, please let us know and we will inform you about the next process.
